About us

Who We Are

KelReport is in business to find the best fashion and lifestyle products Vietnam has to offer. We believe in providing an honest and human approach to fashion and lifestyle.We understand that everyone is busy; they don’t always have time to pore over exhaustive descriptions which can usually be reduced to half the size.

Our team is passionate about what we do and obsessive about scouring the city for the more unique products available right now. KelReport is all about the details, so we focus on the product and the product alone, giving you everything you need to know and even the option to log in and give us your opinion by liking or disliking what we feature.
